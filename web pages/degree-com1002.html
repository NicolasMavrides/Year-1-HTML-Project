    My Degree/COM1002

    COM1002 (Foundations of Computer Science) is a module which covers the Mathematical Theory of Computer Science. The aim of the module is to provide foundational knowledge implemented in Computer Systems and to help develop critical thinking when solving a problem.

    The module has 2 scheduled lectures per week in which the Mathematical theory is covered. A lab session is also held every Thursday, in which an exercise sheet per session is provided to be solved and assessed as part of one's mark.
