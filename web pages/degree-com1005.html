    My Degree/COM1005

    COM1005 (Machines and Intelligence) is a module which covers material on Artificial Intelligence and it's use in Machines. The module has 2 lectures per week in which material on different A.I. theories and historical examples of A.I. are taught.

    The module also offers 1 lab seesion every Friday during which we get to work with practical examples of Artificial Intelligence. For example, plenty of the recent lab sessions have focused on working with NAO Robots (pictured).
